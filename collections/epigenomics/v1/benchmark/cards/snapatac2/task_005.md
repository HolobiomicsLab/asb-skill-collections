## Domain Knowledge
- Motif enrichment p-values reflect the statistical significance of over- or under-representation of DNA motifs in a peak set compared to a background model or null distribution.
- The CIS-BP database is a curated compendium of eukaryotic transcription factor DNA-binding specificity data represented as position-weight matrices.
- Differentially accessible peaks are genomic regions with significantly altered chromatin accessibility between cell populations or conditions, typically derived from ATAC-seq or similar assays.
- Enrichment scores quantify the degree to which a given motif is overrepresented in peak sequences relative to the background, often computed via hypergeometric or Fisher exact test statistics.
- Multiple hypothesis correction (e.g., Benjamini–Hochberg FDR) is typically applied to p-values when testing many motifs simultaneously to control false discovery rate.
