- Checksum verification (e.g., MD5 or SHA256) is the standard reproducibility assurance for ENCODE pipeline outputs, confirming deterministic pipeline execution on identical inputs.
- The pipeline accepts paired-end Hi-C FASTQ files and produces alignment, duplicate filtering, and contact binning steps before generating the final .hic file.
