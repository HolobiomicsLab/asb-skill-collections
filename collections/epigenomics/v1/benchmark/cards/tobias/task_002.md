## Domain Knowledge
- ATAC-seq footprints are visible as depletions of Tn5 insertion sites around transcription factor binding locations due to protein occlusion of the transposase.
- BigWig format stores continuous-valued genomic signal data in a compressed, indexed structure suitable for rapid access and visualization across large genomes.
- Footprint scoring quantifies the magnitude of insertion signal depletion within defined genomic regions, with higher scores indicating stronger TF binding signatures.
- ScoreBigwig generates position-wise scores that reflect local insertion patterns, enabling downstream analysis of binding kinetics and factor occupancy.
