## Domain Knowledge
- ATAC-seq footprints are visible depletions of Tn5 insertions around protein-bound DNA regions, enabling inference of transcription factor occupancy.
- BINDetect requires normalized footprint scores (typically from TOBIAS ScoreBigWig or similar preprocessing) and pre-scanned or provided motif positions to compare binding signal across conditions.
- Occupancy predictions depend on the quality of footprint signal in the input—shallow or noisy footprints reduce discrimination between bound and unbound states.
- Motif databases must be in compatible format (JASPAR PWM, MEME, or TOBIAS-indexed) and should be curated or filtered for the relevant cell type or organism to minimize false-positive predictions.
