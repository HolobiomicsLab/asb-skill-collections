## Domain Knowledge
- TWIM platforms record arrival time (ion detection time) rather than drift time (ion residence time in the mobility cell), requiring conversion to derive CCS values.
- MOCCal performs class assignment and CCS calculations without requiring prior feature identification, enabling analysis of raw or minimally preprocessed multi-omic data.
- CCS calibration establishes the relationship between measured arrival time and known collision cross section values, essential for converting experimental data to absolute CCS.
- Class-specific CCS calculations account for biomolecular class identity (lipids, metabolites, proteins, etc.) to improve accuracy of CCS predictions for high-dimensional multi-omic data.
