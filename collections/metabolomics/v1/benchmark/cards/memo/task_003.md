## Domain Knowledge
- MS2 fingerprints are generated by counting the occurrence of MS2 peaks and neutral losses (mass shifts to the precursor ion) present in each sample's spectra.
- MEMO operates independently of retention time, allowing comparison of samples acquired under different LC conditions or on different mass spectrometer platforms (e.g., MaXis Q-ToF vs. Q-Exactive).
- The MemoMatrix represents sample composition as vectors where each dimension is an MS2 feature (peak or neutral loss) and values are occurrence counts across the sample set.
- Aligned feature tables must be pre-processed and linked to their corresponding MS2 spectra data; matchms and spec2vec handle spectral parsing and document conversion internally within memo-ms.
