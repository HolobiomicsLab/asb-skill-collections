## Domain Knowledge
- Molecular fingerprints are structural representations used for spectral similarity assessment, distinct from peak-based cosine similarity measures.
- Pairwise similarity matrices output one similarity score per spectrum pair, where the matrix is typically symmetric with diagonal values of 1.0 (self-similarity).
- matchms supports multiple spectral input formats; preprocessing and loading must preserve metadata and peak information required for fingerprint computation.
- Fingerprint-based matching is computationally efficient for large-scale spectral library searches compared to peak-intensity methods.
