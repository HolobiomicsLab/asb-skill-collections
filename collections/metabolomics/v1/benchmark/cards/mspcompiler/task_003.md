## Domain Knowledge
- Kovats retention index (RI) is a standardized measure of compound retention time on gas chromatography columns, essential for EI library matching and compound identification.
- Capillary GC columns are the standard platform for modern EI-MS experiments; Lee RI values from packed columns are excluded to ensure data homogeneity.
- When multiple RI records exist for the same compound across different experiments, median RI is used to reduce noise; values with standard deviation exceeding 30 are rejected to exclude outliers.
- The assign_ri function requires specification of column polarity (semi-polar, non-polar, or polar) to match RI data to the appropriate experimental conditions.
