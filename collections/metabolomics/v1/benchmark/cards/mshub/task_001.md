## Domain Knowledge
- GC-MS spectra often contain overlapping peaks from co-eluting compounds; deconvolution resolves each compound's individual mass spectrum using retention time and m/z information.
- Molecular networking in GNPS clusters spectra based on cosine similarity (typically ≥0.7 for positive edges); higher similarity indicates structurally related compounds.
- The Nature Biotechnology 2020 manuscript established reference network topology and deconvolved spectra patterns that serve as the ground truth for this reproduction.
- MSHub auto-deconvolution outputs must be formatted (MGF or mzTab) to be ingested by the GNPS networking pipeline; format mismatches cause pipeline failures.
- Network reproducibility depends on consistent parameter choices (similarity threshold, minimum cluster size, spectral library version) between the original and re-run analyses.
