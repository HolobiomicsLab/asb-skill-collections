## Domain Knowledge
- Lantibiotics are ribosomal peptides with characteristic dehydrated residues (dehydroalanine S-18, dehydrobutyrine T-18) that appear in both raw and modified sequence forms in MetaMiner output.
- antiSMASH .final.gbk files annotate open reading frames and biosynthetic gene clusters but may lose or alter precursor peptide sequence information compared to raw FASTA assemblies due to gene calling or annotation differences.
- AmfS is a known lantibiotic precursor peptide from Streptomyces griseus with the sequence TGSQVSLLVCEYSSLSVVLCTP and its modification pattern T-18GS-18QVS-18LLVCEYS-18SLSVVLCTP, serving as a positive control for lantibiotic detection in this test dataset.
- MetaMiner performs all-vs-all matching between spectra and sequence files by default; output failures may stem from gene discovery or extraction differences between genome mining tool outputs and raw assembly.
- The test data includes parallel runs with FASTA and antiSMASH .gbk inputs on the same biological sample to benchmark input format robustness.
